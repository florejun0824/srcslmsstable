SRCS LMS v15: The Ultimate Design Masterclass 

We are thrilled to introduce the next major evolution of the SRCS Learning Management System. Over the course of 8 comprehensive design iterations, we have meticulously transformed the platform to deliver a stunning, ultra-premium, and universally accessible user experience.

🎨 The New Aesthetic: Bright, Clean, & Glassmorphic
We have officially retired heavy dark mode elements and legacy violet themes to embrace a beautiful, airy, and vibrant pastel aesthetic.
- Premium Typography & Iconography: Standardized all text to crisp Apple system fonts and upgraded the entire app's iconography to high-quality, professional icon sets.
- "Neo-Glass" Components: Replaced standard dark cards with floating glass panels, featuring subtle backdrop blurs, soft layered shadows, and fresh Material Design 3 (MD3) color palettes (Sky, Emerald, Rose, Amber).
- Mobile-First Fluidity: Every view has been heavily optimized for mobile devices, ensuring compact paddings, intuitive headers, and perfectly accessible touch targets.

✨ Key Interface Redesigns
Almost every corner of the LMS has been given a ground-up visual overhaul:
- 🔐 Authentication & Initialization: The Login Page and Post-Login Splash Screens were completely rebuilt from scratch with vivid pastel gradients, smooth animations, and an immersive loading experience.
- 🤖 AI Generation Hub: The Ask AI Chat and Quiz/PPT Generators evolved from transparent layouts into stunning, solid-paneled conversational layouts that feel solid and premium.
- 🆕 What's New Interface: Rebuilt the "What's New" modal into a stunning glassmorphic floating tile. The release notes are now displayed in beautiful hovering micro-cards, featuring smooth, reactive 3D spring animations.
- 📚 Courses & Classes: The Courses and Classes view dynamic grid systems were vastly enhanced with dedicated visually-pleasing subject cards, category arrays, and beautiful empty states.
- 📖 Responsive Unit Accordions: Unit cards now expertly respond to your screen size. On desktop, they retain a gorgeous 4/3 aspect ratio card format. On mobile, they fluidly condense into a sleek horizontal list tile for maximum readability.
- 🗳️ Election & Student Management: Legacy interfaces, modals, and management tabs were replaced with modern blue-gradient architectures, fixing alignment issues like the mobile Election search header.
- 📊 Admin Dashboard Cleanse: Fully audited and deeply cleaned to remove legacy "Cyberpunk" (fuchsia) or violet themes. The administrator telemetry and monitoring views now gracefully fallback to a brilliant sky/blue scale.

🔐 Unified Login Experience
Say goodbye to selecting your role before logging in. Our intelligent authentication system automatically detects whether you're a Student, Teacher, Parent, or Admin. Just enter your credentials, and the system dynamically routes you to your personalized dashboard wrapped in a clean, unified aesthetic.

📊 Advanced Network Activity & Monitoring
For Administrators, visibility is key. We've introduced a comprehensive "Network Activity" dashboard:
- Live Instructor Telemetry: Instantly track teacher engagement, including average logins per week and active screen time.
- Global Metric Tracking: Monitor the total number of lessons posted, quizzes sent, announcements made, and live classes conducted across the entire school network over the last 4 weeks.

⏱️ Precision Session Tracking
We've completely overhauled how we measure engagement. The LMS now accurately tracks the exact time students spend interacting with specific lessons and quizzes, providing teachers and parents with actionable insights into learning habits rather than just simple view counts.

📥 Offline-Ready Learning
Learning doesn't stop when the internet drops. We've introduced persistent offline availability badges on lessons, ensuring students know exactly which materials are cached and accessible anywhere, anytime, without needing a dedicated "Offline" tab.

🛠️ Under-the-Hood Optimizations
- Robust Date Handling: Fortified our data parsing to gracefully handle legacy or missing timestamps, eliminating "Invalid Date" errors in activity logs and gradebooks.
- Memory & Performance: Significant backend refactoring and dependency cleanups ensure lighter initial load sizes and faster screen transitions.
